Using the irobot cloud

Getting your id
===============

You need to get your rommba id and password from irobot.

Follow the steps here : https://github.com/koalazak/dorita980#how-to-find-your-userpassword

Use dolphin to download the root certificate on your phone. It doesn't work with chrome.

You'll find something like :

.. code:: bash

    https://irobot.axeda.com/services/v1/rest/Scripto/execute/AspenApiRequest?blid=xxxxxxxxxxxxx&robotpwd=yyyyyyyyyyyyyyyyyy&method=getStatus
